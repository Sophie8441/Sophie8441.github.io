Slim4

AI-Driven Inventory Management for Optimized B2B Supply Chains

Slim4 by Slimstock is a powerful, AI-based inventory management platform tailored for supply chain optimization within B2B environments.

Designed for inventory managers, demand planners, and production teams, Slim4 enables accurate demand forecasting, inventory planning, and replenishment management, minimizing waste and improving order fulfillment. Its modular structure supports a range of functions—from analytics and production scheduling to order management—offering scalability and adaptability across industries such as retail, manufacturing, food, and e-commerce.

With a role-based authorization system, Slim4 ensures secure and usability access across various user levels, while ERP integration enhances cross-functional collaboration. As a Functional designer working in R&D team, my role focuses on simplifying complex workflows within Slim4’s key modules, making data-driven insights and operational controls accessible and user-friendly for diverse business needs.
