A business website to deliver the branding services and values

A marketing-focused website design&development project

The project was in the collaboration with a local Taiwanese restaurant that aims to launch a real-working website. The web pages function to ensure the delivery of the business services and values to the end users. The client wanted to have a new website that can deliver their high-qulity branding values by using intuitive navigations, nice storytelling, and attractive visuals.

Started with early marketing research, from an in-depth interview, SWOT analysis, and data collected from online survey with feedback from 60+ participants to position the design potential for the business party (restaurant). Design process then followed by information architecture, wireframing UI, and interactive prototyping, to launch a real-functional, RWD website through programing with HTML, CSS and Javascript.

Time frame: 04.2020 - 05.2020

Design Challenge  

The client's challenge was to create a responsive website that displayed the necessary information in an appealing manner. Because the restaurant's location is not very convenient or eye-catching, it would be important to have an appealing way to attract new customers and information presented in an easy-to-understand manner to avoid depressing customers who visit.

Discover 

Face-to-face sessions to concluded the restaurant owner(client)'s needs, siutations and the current challenges and value propositions then followed through the discussion.

Face-to-face discussing with the client

Visual designer presented the proposed visuals to the client

Market Research 

SWOT analysis positioned the client's current business situation from broader perspectives.

The user groups target people who value good and healthy food and enjoy stylish restaurants. Thus, normally there are groups of people coming together to hang out or have chats.

The generated insights from online surveys results were based on 60 participants' responses according to the behaviors and preferences in reviewing the restaurant websites.

Concluded from both the survey and benchmark analysis that most people focused on the useful information (location, costs). Furthermore, the way to present and visualize information were critical.

The benchmark focuses on the restaurants which serve featuristic food and nice restaurant ambiance whose online and offline styles are made consistent and eye-catching. The intersection is focused on the usability of how the user could use the website and the navigation about how the user interface with information.

Define  

In this stage, information architecture was structured to evaluate with the business client for showing the components which were shown important, relevant, useful from the survey with the participants and the valuable insights provided by the client.

information architecture to sketch the components 

Design & Development   

Wireframe sketched to demonstrate the design in interactive stage for triggering better discussion with the client. The construction aimed to deliver the UI in a way that shows information aesthetically appealing catering to the client's branding values and ensures the accessibilty and usability to the customers(end-users).

Website Launch 

The final design is Web UI. The translated UXUI design focus on presenting the important information that the customers would notice in an easy-to-use, consistent and aesthetic way.
