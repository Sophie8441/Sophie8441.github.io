Hi I am Tzu-Jou!🤗

I am a UX UI (product) Designer with experience in user research. I am intereated in researching and bridging users' and customers’ needs and emerging technology for our future every day. I create and polish different ways of experience with aesthetical qualities, a humanistic mindset, and technology-focus with an explorative approach.

Vision in Design. Modern life is increasingly fast-paced, which causes people to experience stress and have difficulty with life choices. This situation emphasizes the potential in triggering a reflective mindset to an individual. This acts as an important role in affectting one's behaviors and lifestyle toward a healthy and desired way (Li, Dey & Forlizzi, 2010). By reassessing and analyzing individualized experiences and relevant contextual information people can be mo􀆟vated to be in charge of the target behaviors/lifestyle (Müller et al., 2012) and can even be supported to experience self-empowerment in being able to make informed decisions (Garcia et al, 2014). In that sense, the designer’s task is to approach individual concerns in interaction with the design to elicit a reflective attitude. One challenge is to design persuasive interventions for supporting behavior change. Taking individual considerations into account. It is an opportunity to motivate individuals to achieve their target behaviors/lifestyle. By using persuasive technologies people can be made aware and even mindful of their behaviors.
